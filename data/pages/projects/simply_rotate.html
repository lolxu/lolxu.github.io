    • Simply Rotate

    Simply Rotate is a physics based puzzle game created in Unity. It was built from one of the projects for the CSCI426 Game Prototyping class I took during my junior year.

    The prototype of Simply Rotate was created to fulfill the theme of wordless gameplay. As designers, we could not use any words to describe or assist players to understand gameplay. Therefore, I turned to simple graphics and different colors to straightforwardly teach players about the gameplay. Most of the simplicity of the gameplay design carried over when we continued to develop the game aftwewards.

    For the engineering side of Simply Rotate, I used C# along with Unity to code the logic of the game. I also compiled a custom retro shader using a custom URP_BlitRenderFeature library for applying a filter effect onto the camera inside the Universal Render Pipeline.

    The game has been submitted to Itch.io and IndieCade 2023.
