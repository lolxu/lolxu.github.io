    • Game Prototypes

    Prototypes and games created from the CSCI426 Game Prototyping class. Every two weeks, we would get a prompt that challenges us to think about a specific design aspect of modern video games. We had themes like: Game Feel, Randomization, Discretization / Continuous feedback, Feedback and Balance, etc. I have uploaded some of the more successful prototypes to Itch.io.

    This course really challenged me to think about the design aspect of video games. Having constraints and limited scopes helped me to squeeze out as much creativity I can when creating the prototypes.

    I used the Unity Engine and C# to code these prototypes. I used simple shapes and art to make these prototypes feasible for the two-week development time. I am also a fan of simplistic graphics :D
